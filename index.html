Best Practices
One key per tester. Never share the same key between multiple testers — you can't track usage or revoke individually.
Revoke in Groq console. The expiry date on the card is informational. Real revocation is at console.groq.com/keys.
Test Focus field helps you remember what each tester was evaluating when you review the ledger later.
Re-issue button in the ledger loads a tester back into the form for a fresh card in 2 clicks.
Never share production keys. Always create a dedicated key per tester session.
